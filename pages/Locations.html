Find Us On The Map

  • From Addis Ababa Bole International Airport, take the road toward Woliso — approximately 2.5 hours by car.
  • Driving from Addis Ababa via the Addis Ababa–Woliso road, the hotel is located near the town center.
  • Regular intercity buses and minibuses run between Addis Ababa and Woliso throughout the day.
  • Complimentary airport shuttle service is available for guests — contact the front desk to arrange pickup.
